BaseLib is a mod for Slay the Spire 2 intended to help with and standardize the addition of content.

You can add BaseLib as a dependency to your mod through NuGet.

`<PackageReference Include="Alchyr.Sts2.BaseLib" Version="*" />`

You are also suggested to use `Alchyr.Sts2.ModAnalyzers` which will help with localization.
